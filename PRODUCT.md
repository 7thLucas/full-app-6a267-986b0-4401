# MEX — Video Analyzer & Transcription App

## Product Overview
MEX is a web application that enables users to upload, analyze, and transcribe videos. It focuses on making video content accessible and searchable through accurate transcription and intelligent analysis.

## Target Users
- Content creators who need transcripts of their videos
- Researchers and educators working with video content
- Businesses that need to extract insights from recorded meetings, webinars, or training videos
- Media professionals needing quick access to video content text

## Brand & Tone
- Name: MEX
- Tone: Professional, efficient, modern
- Feel: Clean and minimal — focused on productivity and clarity
- Anti-references: Avoid cluttered interfaces, overly playful design, or consumer-app aesthetics

## Core Features
- Video upload and management
- Automatic transcription of video content
- Transcription viewer with timestamp navigation
- Search within transcriptions
- Admin dashboard for managing users and processing jobs
- Role-based access control (Admin, User)

## Strategic Principles
- Speed and accuracy are paramount
- Transcriptions should be easy to read, export, and search
- The interface should get out of the way — content is king
- Scalable from single users to enterprise teams
