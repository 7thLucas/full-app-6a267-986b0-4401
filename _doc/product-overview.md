# Product Overview: MEX

## What It Is
MEX is a full-stack SaaS web application that allows users to upload, process, and gain AI-powered insights from video content. It provides automated transcription, scene detection, sentiment analysis, and content summarization — turning raw video files into structured, searchable, and actionable intelligence.

## Users & Personas
- **Content Creators**: YouTubers, podcasters, and educators who want automatic transcripts, chapter markers, and highlight clips from their video content.
- **Business Analysts**: Operations and marketing teams who need to analyze video meetings, customer interviews, and sales calls for key moments and sentiment signals.
- **Developers / Admins**: Platform administrators who manage users, monitor usage, configure system settings, and review analytics dashboards.

## Core Features
- **Video Upload & Management**: Upload videos via browser, receive processing status in real time, and manage a personal media library.
- **AI Transcription**: Automatic speech-to-text transcription with speaker diarization and timestamped text output.
- **Scene & Object Detection**: Frame-level analysis identifying scene changes, key objects, and visual events throughout the video.
- **Sentiment & Tone Analysis**: Automated sentiment scoring across the timeline to identify emotional peaks, tone shifts, and audience engagement moments.
- **Content Summarization**: AI-generated summaries and chapter breakdowns that condense long videos into scannable highlights.
- **Search & Filtering**: Full-text search across transcripts and metadata, with filters by date, duration, speaker, and sentiment score.
- **Admin Dashboard**: RBAC-based admin interface for user management, usage metrics, system configuration, and audit logs.

## Positioning
Video Analyzer positions itself as the intelligent backend for video-heavy workflows — bridging the gap between raw recorded content and structured business intelligence. It is a developer-friendly SaaS template and product that teams can deploy and extend.

## Brand & Tone
- **Brand name**: Video Analyzer
- **Tone**: Professional, precise, and quietly powerful. The product is calm and analytical — not flashy.
- **Visual style**: Clean, data-forward, with a dark-tinted primary palette and crisp white surfaces.
- **Brand colors**:
  - Primary: `#1E293B` (slate-800, deep navy)
  - Secondary: `#6366F1` (indigo-500, focus accent)
  - Accent: `#38BDF8` (sky-400, data highlight)

## Scope
- In scope: video upload, AI analysis pipeline, transcription, scene detection, sentiment analysis, summarization, admin dashboard, RBAC.
- Out of scope: live video streaming, video editing, social sharing, mobile native apps.

## Strategic Principles
1. Every insight must be grounded in the video's actual content — no hallucinated summaries.
2. The admin layer is a first-class citizen: visibility, control, and audit trails matter.
3. The product is a deployable template — extensibility and modularity are core values.
