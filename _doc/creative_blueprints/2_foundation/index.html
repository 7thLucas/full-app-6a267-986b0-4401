The Problem & Solution

The Problem

Video content is rich — but almost entirely unsearchable.

Teams record hours of meetings, interviews, and training sessions but have no practical way to extract meaning. Reviewing footage manually is slow, costly, and inconsistent. Valuable signals — sentiment shifts, key decisions, recurring topics — stay buried.

The Solution

Video Analyzer turns recordings into structured intelligence — automatically.

Upload a video and receive a full transcript with speaker labels, scene-by-scene breakdowns, sentiment timelines, and an AI-generated summary — all searchable, shareable, and ready to act on. No manual review required.
